🌾 Part 1: Supporting Local Products
🧺 What Are Local Products?

Local products are goods made in our country using local resources and workers.

🌾 Farm Products 🌴 Forest Products 🏠 Home Industry Products
👉 These help people earn a living and strengthen the Philippine economy.
🚜 Role of Farmers & Agricultural Workers
  • Grow crops for food and income
  • Produce raw materials
  • Help sustain communities
⚠️ Problems Faced by Farmers
  1. Lack of money (capital)
  2. Need more land and higher production
  3. Old or poor machinery
  4. Expensive fertilizers and pesticides
  5. Need better seeds
  6. Natural disasters (e.g., drought)
  7. Low profit despite big harvests
🌾 Secondary Crops

Grown in addition to main crops to provide extra income and food supply.

🧄 Garlic 🧅 Onions 🥜 Peanuts
✔ Extra income  ✔ More food supply  ✔ Used for other products
🥜 Importance of Peanuts

Peanuts can be eaten or processed into many products:

🍬 Candies 🧴 Creams 🥜 Butter

The stems can be used as animal food. A source of income and industry.

👩‍🌾 Helps farmers & workers 💪 Strengthens economy 🇵🇭 Promotes Filipino products 💼 Creates jobs 📦 Reduces imports

🌍 Part 2: Sustainable Development
🌏 What is Sustainable Development?

Development that meets the needs of the present without harming the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

👥 People 🌍 Planet 💰 Prosperity ☮️ Peace
🌟 Why is Sustainable Development Important?
  • ✔ Protects the environment
  • ✔ Reduces poverty
  • ✔ Improves quality of life
  • ✔ Ensures future resources
  • ✔ Promotes peace and equality
🏡 Activities: Community & Health
  • Keep community clean
  • Ensure clean water and sanitation
  • Promote healthy environments
⚖️ Social Responsibility
  • Respect human rights
  • Fight corruption
  • Promote fairness and equality
💼 Economic Actions
  • Support SDG-aligned businesses
  • Partner with local businesses
  • Invest in long-term projects
🌞 Environmental Protection
  • Use renewable energy: solar ☀️, wind 💨, biofuel 🌿
  • Protect nature and wildlife

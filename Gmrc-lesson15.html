People of God:
Priest, Prophet, and King

Interactive Reviewer · Things to Remember & Assessment

✦
📌 Things to Remember
  • 1All Christians are also called to serve in God's name and for the salvation of others.
  • 2We became a member of the People of God during baptism.
  • 3As the People of God, our head is Jesus Christ.
  • 4Jesus Christ is anointed by the Father with the Holy Spirit and is established as priest, prophet, and king.
  • 5As the People of God, we share in Jesus' priesthood.
  • 6Our acts of charity make us "another Christ" for other people.
  • 7To practice our "common priesthood" means to represent Jesus Christ to the world faithfully.
  • 8As the People of God who share Jesus' priesthood, we are all called to offer spiritual sacrifices.
  • 9We can seek and receive God's help through the sacraments.
  • 10In the Holy Mass, the Church gathers to remember and to represent the sacrifice of Christ, which we share through the action of the priest and the power of the Holy Spirit.

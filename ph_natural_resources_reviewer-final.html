🌱 1. Land Resources ▼
⚠️ Problems with Land
🧪
Too Much Fertilizer
Using too many chemicals pollutes the soil and water, and hurts the tiny living things in the soil.
🌊
Soil Erosion
Wind and water wash away the top layer of soil, making it hard to grow plants and crops.
🐄
Overgrazing
When too many animals eat grass faster than it can grow back, the land becomes bare and dry.
🧂
Salinization
Too much watering leaves salt in the soil. The salt ruins the land and plants can no longer grow.
✅ How to Take Care of Land
1
🌿 Use Organic Fertilizer — Use compost, worm castings, or animal manure instead of chemicals to feed the soil naturally.
2
🌾 Strip Cropping — Plant different crops in strips side by side. This slows down water and stops soil from washing away.
3
😴 Fallowing — Let the land rest for one season with no crops planted so it can get its nutrients back.
🌲 2. Forest Resources ▼
⚠️ Problems with Forests
🔥
Kaingin (Slash-and-Burn)
Farmers cut and burn trees to clear land for farming. This destroys animal homes and damages the soil.
🪓
Logging & Charcoal Making
Cutting too many trees and burning wood to make charcoal takes away the forest much faster than it can grow back.
✅ DENR's 8 Rules to Protect Forests
🏛️DENR = Department of Environment and Natural Resources — the government group that protects our forests.
1
🪵 Selective Logging — Only cut old, fully grown trees. Leave the young ones to keep growing.
2
🌳 Reforestation — Plant new trees to replace the ones that were cut down.
3
🚫 Ban on Log Exports — Stop selling our logs and lumber to other countries so we keep more trees here.
4
🔥 No Kaingin or Charcoal Making — Slash-and-burn farming and making charcoal from wood are both against the law.
5
🎋 Non-Wood Industries — Use bamboo and rattan instead of cutting trees for wood products.
6
🦅 Protect Forest Animals — Make sure all animals living in the forest are kept safe from harm.
7
🔬 Artificial Insemination — Help endangered animals have more babies so their families don't disappear.
8
🛡️ Protected Areas — Mark special forests as protected zones where no one can cut or harm them.
💧 3. Water Resources ▼
⚠️ Problems with Water
🗑️
Throwing Trash in Waterways
Garbage dumped in rivers and canals pollutes our water and causes floods in communities.
🏭
Factory Waste & Mine Tailings
Dirty water from factories and mines contains dangerous chemicals that poison rivers and groundwater.
🚱
Unsafe Drinking Water
Polluted water makes people sick. Many communities still don't have clean water to drink.
✅ How to Take Care of Water
1
🚯 No Dumping in Waterways — It is against the law to throw garbage into rivers, canals, and the sea.
2
🧹 Clean Canals & Esteros — The government regularly cleans waterways to prevent floods.
3
🏗️ Wastewater Treatment — Businesses must clean their dirty water before releasing it into waterways.
4
📜 R.A. 9275 — Clean Water Act of 2004 — This law protects all of the Philippines' water resources and punishes polluters.
💡 Remember: R.A. 9275 = Philippine Clean Water Act, passed in 2004!
🐟 4. Marine Resources ▼
⚠️ Problems in the Ocean
💣
Dynamite & Cyanide Fishing
These illegal fishing methods destroy coral reefs and kill all sea creatures nearby, not just the fish being caught.
🕸️
Drift-Net Fishing
Giant nets trap and kill dolphins, sea turtles, and other sea animals that were not supposed to be caught.
🏗️
Coastal Construction
Building near the shore destroys mangroves and coral reefs — the places where young fish live and hide.
🛢️
Garbage & Oil Spills
Plastic waste and oil from ships kill sea animals, ruin coral reefs, and make the seafood we eat unsafe.
✅ How to Take Care of Our Seas (BFAR Programs)
🐠BFAR = Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources — the government group that takes care of our fish and seas.
1
⚓ Bantay Dagat (Sea Watch) — Community patrols that watch over the sea and catch people who fish illegally.
2
🚤 Provide Patrol Boats & Equipment — Give coast guards the tools they need to stop illegal fishing.
3
🌊 Promote Offshore Fishing — Fish far from the shore so coral reefs near the coast can rest and recover.
4
🏝️ Declare Protected Sea Areas — Set aside parts of the ocean where fishing and construction are not allowed.
5
🔬 Fish Breeding Programs — Raise fish in safe places and release them into the sea to grow their numbers.
💨 5. Air Resources ▼
⚠️ What Causes Air Pollution?
🚗
Vehicle Smoke
Cars, trucks, and jeepneys release dirty smoke that contains harmful gases like carbon monoxide.
🗑️
Burning Trash
When garbage is burned instead of properly disposed of, it releases toxic fumes into the air.
🌡️
Aerosols & CFCs
Spray cans and old refrigerators release chemicals that punch holes in the ozone layer — our shield against the sun.
📢
Noise Pollution
Very loud sounds from vehicles and factories are also a type of pollution that harms people and animals.
🌡️ What Happens Because of Air Pollution?
🌏 Greenhouse Effect — Dirty gases like CO₂ act like a blanket around the Earth, trapping heat and making the planet warmer. This is called global warming.
🌀
Stronger Typhoons
Warmer oceans make typhoons bigger and more powerful — which is very dangerous for the Philippines!
🌊
Climate Change
Seasons change, sea levels rise, and weather becomes extreme — all because of too much pollution in the air.
✅ What We Can Do
1
🌳 Plant Trees — Trees absorb CO₂ from the air and give us clean oxygen to breathe.
2
🚿 Avoid Aerosols & CFCs — Use eco-friendly products that don't destroy the ozone layer.
3
☀️ Use Renewable Energy — Switch to solar, wind, or water power instead of burning coal or oil.
⛽ 6. Nonrenewable Resources ▼
🔑 Nonrenewable means these resources cannot be replaced once they are used up. They took millions of years to form underground!
📦 Examples of Nonrenewable Resources
⛏️ Coal 🛢️ Petroleum (Oil) 🔥 Natural Gas 💎 Minerals ⚙️ Metal Ores
⚠️ Why Are They a Problem?
💨
Greenhouse Gases
Burning fossil fuels releases CO₂ and other gases that cause the greenhouse effect and climate change.
⏳
Running Out
Once we use them all up, they are gone forever. We must use them wisely and find alternatives.
⛏️
Mining Damage
Digging for coal and minerals destroys land, pollutes nearby rivers, and harms animal habitats.
☀️
Use Renewables Instead!
Solar, wind, water, and geothermal energy are clean alternatives that will never run out.
🤝 7. Good Community Practices ▼
💛 Things WE Can Do to Help!
♻️
Segregate Your Trash
Sort garbage into paper 📄, plastic 🧴, glass 🍾, and metal 🥫 bins. Don't mix them up!
💡
Save Water & Electricity
Turn off the faucet when not in use. Switch off the lights when you leave a room. Every bit helps!
🔄
Recycle
Reuse old materials like bottles, paper, and cans so we don't have to keep making brand-new ones from scratch.
🌳
Join Clean-up Drives
Participate in tree-planting, coastal clean-ups, and environmental campaigns in your community!

🐟
